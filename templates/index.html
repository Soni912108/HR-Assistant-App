How HR Assistant Works:

  1. Upload your PDF documents.
  2. Provide keywords or instructions to guide the Assistant.
  3. Ask questions about the content of the PDFs using the chat interface.
  4. HR Assistant analyzes the documents and delivers relevant answers.

Example Hints

Here are some hints to give to the Assistant:

  • "Given a PDF file with CVs, identify candidates with experience in Python and project management."

Examples Questions

Ask questions like:

  • "Summarize the key qualifications required for the Marketing Manager position."
  • "Extract the employee benefits information from the company handbook."
